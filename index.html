#2About

Three quick things about me and my work:

  1. In 2019, I graduated from the International Baccalaureate® (IB) Diploma Programme (DP) in Dubai. Now, I'm pursuing a BFA in Communication Design at Parsons School of Design in New York City.

  2. I care most about the people I work with and how that collaboration is experienced by all involved. A belief deeply fostered by my years spent living abroad in nine countries and counting.

  3. When I'm not hunched over my laptop and playing with Adobe CS, I'm either reading a fantasy book, writing about robots, or taking pictures of my dog for the hundredth time.
